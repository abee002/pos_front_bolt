You are designing a modern **Point of Sale (POS) System** with a **dark theme UI**.  
The design must be optimized for quick retail usage, touch screens, and real-time transactions.  

🎨 Color Palette:
- Primary Background: #222021  
- Secondary Background: #86837B  
- Accent Shades: #808588, #BDB7AB  
- Text: White and light grays for high readability  

💡 Guidelines:
- Use #222021 as the main app background (dashboard, product catalog, billing screen).  
- Panels, modals, and sidebar navigation should use #86837B to create separation.  
- Use #808588 for hover states, inactive buttons, and table row alternates.  
- Use #BDB7AB for highlights, dividers, and subtle borders.  
- Typography: bold white for key numbers (totals, quantities), light gray for secondary info.  
- Buttons (Checkout, Pay, Add to Cart): solid or gradient (#86837B → #BDB7AB) with white text.  
- Touch-friendly design: large buttons with rounded corners and spacing.  
- Product cards: dark panels (#86837B) with product image, name, and price in light text.  
- Tables (order history, receipts): alternating rows #222021 and #808588 for clarity.  
- Notifications (payment success, error, low stock) → use accent colors with strong contrast (green, yellow, red).  

Overall vibe → **sleek, professional dark mode POS interface, designed for speed, clarity, and usability in low-light retail environments.**
